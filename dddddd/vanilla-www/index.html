MTG Life Counter: Lotus

Lotus is the all-in-one Magic: The Gathering life counter and companion app. Track life totals, commander damage, and every counter you need, roll any die, search cards with prices and legality, and keep the game on pace with built-in timers.

Lotus is available on the Web, in the iOS App Store and on Google Play.

MTG Companion App Features

  • Commander-ready MTG life tracker: Adjust life totals instantly, monitor commander damage, and keep partner commanders in check across pods of up to 10 players.
  • All the counters you care about: Toggle poison, energy, experience, storm, charge, and custom counters to stay organized.
  • Built-in MTG card search: Look up any card with pricing, format legality, and printings to settle rules questions or plan upgrades mid-game.
  • Fast dice rolls and coin flips: Access D4 through D20 rolls, high-roll tiebreakers, and quick coin flips without shuffling between apps.
  • Game timer and turn tracker: Track total play time and individual turns to keep Commander nights moving and avoid slow play.
  • Make it yours: Make every game feel personal with custom backgrounds, victory and defeat messages, and more.

How to use

Here’s how to use LifeCounter.app to track life, commander damage, and more;

  1. Swipe left or right on the player card to track commander damage. Perfect for tracking those pesky Voltron commanders!
  2. Swipe up or down on the player card to access customization options. Here’s where the magic happens:
    • Personalize the background color or set a custom image that reflects your favorite commander or card.
    • Enable partner commanders for those dynamic duos that double the fun—and the trouble.
    • Kill and unkill a player: Only one may stand!
    • Track a variety of counters, including Commander Tax, Poison, Storm, Mana, Experience, and more.
  3. Long tap on the increase/decrease buttons for fast life count adjustments, jumping by 10 at a time. Perfect for when you’re gaining life faster than an Ajani’s Pridemate on steroids or losing life quicker than someone facing down a Blightsteel Colossus.
  4. High Roll: Roll a d20 and highlight the winning roll. Perfect for determining who goes first without any arguments.
  5. Support for up to 10 players: Yes, you can manage life totals for up to 10 players. Just a heads up, if you’re playing with this many people, expect the game to take at least a weekend (or two).
  6. Turn Tracker: Keep tabs on the turn count and even track how long each player spends strategizing (or overthinking) during their turn.
  7. Game Time Tracker: Track your game duration with a simple stopwatch function that can be paused and resumed.
  8. Manual Life Input: Tap the life total display to bring up a numeric keypad for precise adjustments. Perfect for those “oops, I just went infinite” moments.
  9. Integrated Card Database:
    • Search any Magic card instantly
    • View all available printings and current market prices
    • Check format legality
    • Perfect for settling rules disputes or finding that perfect upgrade for your deck

Pro Tip: Add to Home screen

Adding LifeCounter.app to your home screen can enhance your experience, giving you quick and easy access and allow you to view it in fullscreen. Here’s how you can do it:

For iOS:

  1. Open Safari and navigate to LifeCounter.app.
  2. Tap the Share icon (the square with an upward arrow) at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Select “Add to Home Screen”.
  4. Tap “Add” in the upper right corner, and you’re all set!

For Android:

  1. Open Chrome and go to LifeCounter.app.
  2. Tap the Menu icon (three vertical dots) in the top right corner.
  3. Select “Add to Home screen”.
  4. Confirm by tapping “Add”, and you’re ready to go!

Now, with LifeCounter.app just a tap away, you can keep your games running smoothly. May your draws be ever in your favor!
