## Mulligan Analysis

Lorehold is a 5cmc commander. Ideal opening hands:
- 3-4 lands (at least 1 RW dual or fetch)
- 1-2 ramp pieces (Sol Ring, Signet, Talisman, Ritual)
- 1-2 cheap spells to trigger Lorehold
- Curve topping at 3-4cmc

**Keep:** 3 lands + Sol Ring + 2-drop
**Keep:** 3 lands + Land Tax + interaction
**Keep:** 3 lands + Signet + Sensei's Top + 3-drop
**Mulligan:** 0-1 lands, 6+ lands, all 5+ cmc spells, single color

## Lorehold Strategy

1. Cast a cheap spell first, then a big spell second to copy with Lorehold
2. Topdeck manipulation: Top, Scroll Rack, Valakut Awakening
3. Ritual into big spells: Desperate Ritual -> Seething Song -> Jeska's Will
4. Copy payoffs: Double Vision, Reverberate, Flare of Duplication
5. Win via: copied big spells, Insurrection, Storm Herd, creature beats
